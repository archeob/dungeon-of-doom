Place sound effect files here (.wav or .ogg recommended).
The filename without extension is the sound name used in code.

Required files (game will run silently without them):
  hit_player.wav        — player takes damage
  hit_monster.wav       — player lands a hit
  kill_monster.wav      — monster is slain
  player_death.wav      — player dies
  level_up.wav          — XP level gained
  pickup.wav            — item picked up
  equip.wav             — item equipped / unequipped
  drink_potion.wav      — potion consumed
  read_scroll.wav       — scroll read
  zap_wand.wav          — wand fired
  eat_food.wav          — food eaten
  stairs_down.wav       — descend staircase
  stairs_up.wav         — ascend staircase
  victory.wav           — escaped with Orb of Carnos
  gate_keeper.wav       — Gate Keeper spawns
  boulder_push.wav      — boulder pushed
  curse_blocked.wav     — cursed item prevents action
  remove_curse.wav      — Remove Curse scroll used
  wand_empty.wav        — wand out of charges
  potion_bad.wav        — harmful potion consumed
